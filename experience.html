My Experience in Singapore Polytechnic

My journey in Singapore Polytechnic (SP) has been wonderful so far. Each modules are fun in their own ways and the lecturers always ensures that the lessons are interesting so as to not make us bored. All the facilicties are well-kept and I had trouble using any of them. The foodcourts (all 6 of them) have a variety of mouth-watering food. Everyone here in SP - students, lecturers, hawker and other staff members - are kind-hearted and are always willing to help you. SP itself is an incredibly big school; walking from point A to point B can be tiring sometime. On the bright side, it counts for some great exercise! All in all, my experience in SP so far has been lovely and I am looking forward to making more grand memories.
